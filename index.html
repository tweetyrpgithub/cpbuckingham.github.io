About

I work as an Instructor for Galvanize helping people find their careers in the techology sector. I work with talented and dedicated people here in Austin, helping to provide relevant ciriculum to avid tech enthusiasts and bring communities together.


Before ending up in Texas, I worked as a Java Developer with American Express on Big Data Smart Offer Platform in Phoenix.

My previous work experience includes LivingSocial, United States Coast Guard, and R.R. Donnelley.


My programming experience includes Java, RoR, NodeJS, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, SQL Databases, TDD (RSpec, Capybara, Selenium), RESTful APIs, Version Control (Git, SVN), Agile Practices through Rally and Pivotal Tracker.
